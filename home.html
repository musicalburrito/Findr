About this Website

Our website is a resource for people to discover areas in the United States and view the housing price to salary ratio for them.

This is useful as while searching for an area to work in, the salary is not the only consideration in mind. Other factors, such as cost of living and housing must be taken into account.

Our tool provides job seekers with a resource to compare the ratios of the average salary for a user-chosen industry to the average house cost in an area.
